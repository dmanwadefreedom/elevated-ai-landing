The Problem Most Agents Don't See

Right now, 97% of realtors are fighting over the same tiny slice of the market — the 3-7% of people actively searching for an agent.

These are the leads on Zillow. Realtor.com. HomeAdvisor. The ones filling out forms at 2am and getting called by 5, 6, sometimes 7 different agents before breakfast.

You know exactly what that looks like. Price wars. Race to the bottom. Competing on commission cuts instead of value.

Then there's the other stuff. Door knocking. Open houses. Begging past clients for referrals. Posting on social media and hoping someone in your sphere decides to sell.

Some of it works. Sometimes. But it doesn't scale. It breaks the minute you stop doing it. And it keeps you trapped on the hamster wheel.

"If your pipeline depends on who you know or who calls you back, you don't have a pipeline. You have a prayer."

There's a better way. And almost nobody in real estate is doing it yet.

What We Do Differently

We don't go after the 3-7%. We target the other 80% of the market — homeowners who are open to selling but aren't actively looking for a realtor.

Think about it. People don't wake up and decide to sell their house. Life happens first.

They just got married. Got divorced. Had a baby. Changed jobs. Retired. Or they're part of the silver tsunami — boomers sitting in 4-bedroom houses their kids moved out of years ago, wondering if now's the time to downsize.

These people aren't on Zillow. They're not filling out forms. They're not being contacted by 7 agents. Most of them haven't even talked to one agent yet.

We reach them with education-angle content. Not "Looking to sell? Call us!" — that's what everyone else does. Instead, it's things like:

Example Ad Angles:

  • • "Why 2025 might be the best year to downsize (4 reasons)"
  • • "3 things empty nesters wish they knew before listing"
  • • "Just had a baby? Here's why your starter home might be costing you"

The result? When these people are ready to talk to an agent, you're the first and only agent they talk to.

No competition. No bidding wars. No race to the bottom.
