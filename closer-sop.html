Closer SOP

Exclusive Brokerage / Elevated AI Solutions — Pay-Per-Appointment Lead Gen for Real Estate Agents

0 Before the Call — Pre-Frame System

  • Landing page → prospect books a call
  • Thank you page plays an FAQ video (handles pricing, guarantee, how-it-works objections before you ever speak)
  • "The Doc" sent via text after booking — written sales letter explaining what we do, how, and why
  • Boot camp touchpoints — educational content keeps them warm between booking and call
  • Value-based follow-ups (not spammy) — multiple touches before the call

Why This Matters

  • By the time they get on the phone, they should already understand the offer
  • Your job is NOT to pitch from scratch — it's to confirm fit and close
  • The pre-frame does 60-70% of the selling for you

1 Connect

  • Connect over background, location, something lighthearted
  • DON'T ask dumb icebreakers ("How's the weather?")
  • Be human. Be real. Build rapport fast — 60 seconds max

2 Context

"Did you make time to go through the information I sent? Give me a breakdown — or are you coming in fresh?"
  • Reviewed "Love it. That's one thing we look for in a partner — someone going out of their way. What stood out to you?"
  • Didn't review "Off to a rough start already" (playful, not aggressive — creates accountability)

3 Intro

"What we see with a lot of real estate agents is they work hard, have great referral networks, but are missing full control of qualified appointments every week. They've tried Zillow, Realtor.com, running ads, door knocking — works on and off but breaks. We use targeted advertising, AI appointment setting, and trained follow-up systems to generate qualified leads AND nurture and close them."

4 Frame

"We can't help everyone. Not everyone is ready. This call is a deeper dive to see if there's alignment. If there is, I'll show you how to get started today. If not, I'll explain why and give my honest advice. Sound fair?"
  • Sets you as the selector, not the seller
  • Eliminates "let me think about it" — decision happens today

5 Discovery

Draw Out These 3 Things

  • Problem/Goal awareness — What are they trying to solve? Where do they want to be?
  • Timeline + Numbers — How long have they been stuck? What have they spent? How many deals are they doing now vs. what they want?
  • Marketing complexity — Get them to acknowledge they can't just "throw up ads." If it were easy, they'd have done it already

6 Education Pitch — Sell the System

Mechanism 1: Problem State Ads

  • 80% of homeowners are in a PROBLEM STATE (need to sell, relocate, downsize, divorce, inheritance)
  • Only 3% of realtors target these people
  • Everyone else competes for the 3-7% in SOLUTION STATE (actively searching Zillow)
  • Education-angle headlines: "Why 2025 is the best time to downsize"
  • You're fishing where nobody else is fishing

Mechanism 2: Pre-Nurture System

  • Education funnel builds trust BEFORE they talk to the agent
  • Triple qualification process — by the time they get on the phone, they're pre-sold
  • You're the ONLY agent they're talking to (no Zillow lead shared with 10 agents)
  • Exclusive appointments, not shared leads

Mechanism 3: AI Follow-Up Engine

  • Gets leads OFF Facebook fast — prevents competitors from retargeting them
  • 6 specialized AI agents handle each stage: qualification, booking, reminders, follow-up, re-engagement, nurture
  • Old leads compound into next month — your pipeline grows, it never starts from zero
  • Works 24/7 while you focus on closing

7 Push Away

"I can't close deals for you. Off $1K-$1.5K ad spend we typically generate enough leads for 3-4 home appointments, 1-2 close. How confident are you in closing these types of leads?"
  • Forces them to self-qualify
  • Creates ownership — you bring the leads, they bring the skill
  • If they hesitate here, this is a coaching moment, not a disqualifier

8 Transition

"Does this excite you? What were the 2-3 biggest clicks for you?"
"Any parts that make you feel uncertain?"
  • Gets them to verbalize value (they sell themselves)
  • Surfaces hidden objections before the close

9 Pitch 2 — Deliverables, Timeline, Close

  • Onboarding + ad account access setup
  • Reactivation campaign first — mine their existing database for quick wins
  • Ads launched Week 1 — first leads within 3 days
  • Weekly check-ins to review performance and optimize
  • Follow-up agents stay on leads that aren't ready now (compounding pipeline)
"Any questions, or are you ready to rock?"

10 Objection Handling — CIVR Framework

The 4-Step Loop

  • C — Clarify: "Help me understand what you mean by that"
  • I — Isolate: "Is that the only thing holding you back, or is there something else?"
  • V — Validate: "That makes total sense. A lot of our best clients said the same thing"
  • R — Reframe: Redirect to value, timeline urgency, or cost of inaction

5 Areas of Confusion That Block Closes

  • Goal clarity — They don't know what they actually want. Help them define it
  • Vehicle buy-in — They don't believe paid lead gen works. Use the Problem State vs. Solution State education
  • Delivery method trust — They've been burned by agencies before. Explain the AI system difference
  • Personal trust — They don't trust YOU yet. Use testimonials, case studies, specificity
  • Value vs. price — They think it's expensive. Reframe: what's one closed home worth? $8K-$15K commission vs. your monthly investment

+ After the Call

  • Follow up with value, not desperation. Send a case study, a relevant insight, a resource
  • Stay unemotional. Trust the process. Not every call closes same-day
  • Some people buy later. Good product + good offer = they come back. Your job is to stay top of mind
  • Never chase. Position yourself as the opportunity they either take or miss
