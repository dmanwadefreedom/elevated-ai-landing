Common Questions

Will LinkedIn flag or ban my account?

No. Our AI operates within LinkedIn's daily limits and mimics natural human behavior patterns. We've processed over 50,000 connections without a single account restriction. We also use warm-up sequences for newer accounts.

How personalized are the messages really?

Every message is unique. The AI reads the prospect's headline, about section, recent posts, shared connections, and company info. It writes messages that reference specific details — not "I noticed your profile" generic outreach.

Can I review messages before they're sent?

Yes. You can run in "approval mode" where you review and approve each message. Most brokers start there, then switch to fully autonomous within a week once they see the quality.

How does the AI voice agent work?

When a LinkedIn prospect shows buying signals, the AI can make a phone call using a natural-sounding voice. It has a real conversation, qualifies the lead, handles common objections, and books a meeting on your calendar — all in real time.

What if I target multiple niches?

No problem. You can run multiple campaigns simultaneously — investors, developers, relocators, commercial tenants — each with their own messaging strategy and qualification criteria.

How fast will I see results?

Most brokers see their first booked meeting within 5-7 days. By week 3, the system is fully optimized and meetings are coming in consistently. The AI gets smarter over time as it learns what messaging works best for your market.
