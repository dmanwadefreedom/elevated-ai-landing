FORMULA #2: STAT SHOCK

"78% of buyers go with the FIRST agent who responds. Here's why you're losing."

Winning Patterns Across Top Ads

Common patterns found across the best-performing real estate lead gen ads on Facebook/Instagram in 2026.

1. Lead Form Ads Beat Landing Pages

Facebook's native Lead Form Ads (Instant Forms) consistently outperform ads that send traffic to external landing pages. Prefilled fields (name, email) reduce friction. Completion rates are significantly higher. Most top performers use this format for home valuations and buyer guides.

2. Video Outperforms Static 2-to-1

Video ads generate 2x more inquiries than static image ads. Agent-narrated walkthroughs, UGC-style talking head videos, and screen recordings of AI systems in action all perform well. Keep videos under 90 seconds. Hook in first 3 seconds.

3. Hyper-Local Targeting Wins

Top ads target specific zip codes, neighborhoods, or even streets. Generic city-wide targeting costs 3-5x more per lead. Use neighborhood names in headlines. Reference local landmarks, schools, or market stats. Facebook allows zip code targeting within Meta Ads Manager.

4. The "Free Report" Offer Dominates

The most common high-performing offer is a free home valuation report or free buyer guide. These low-commitment offers convert at $4-$15 CPL. The key: avoid asking "Ready to sell?" (high commitment). Instead ask "Curious what your home is worth?" (low commitment, high curiosity).

5. Speed-to-Lead Is the Differentiator

78% of buyers work with the first agent who responds. The best lead gen companies emphasize sub-60-second follow-up via AI text. This is Conor's biggest selling point -- his AI agents respond in under a minute. Lead it with in every ad.

6. Carousel Format for Listings

Carousel ads (4-6 images) are the top format for individual listings. The swiping action keeps users engaged longer. Use best photos only. First image should be the hero exterior shot. Last slide should be the CTA. Ylopo's Dynamic Ads automate this from MLS data.

7. Problem-State vs Solution-State (Cortana Framework)

80% of the market is "open to change" but not actively searching for a solution. Only 3-7% are solution-aware. 97% of agents fight over that tiny pool. Conor's ads should target problem-state homeowners with education-angle messaging. This is the Cortana AI Accelerator framework and it's the biggest strategic advantage.
